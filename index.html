Our Story

The man behind the scent

Maaz Safder grew up surrounded by the rich fragrance culture of Pakistan — where oud burners light homes at dawn and attar is as much a ritual as it is a luxury.

What started as a personal obsession became a craft: hand-blending, testing, and perfecting formulas that honour Eastern tradition while speaking the language of modern luxury.

Every Maaz Perfumes bottle is a small-batch creation. No shortcuts. No synthetic filler. Just ingredients that respect your skin and your story.
